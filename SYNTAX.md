**1. Heading**  
---
**Syntax :**  
```
# Heading 1  
## Heading 2  
### Heading 3  
#### Heading 4  
```
**Output :**    
# Heading 1  
## Heading 2  
### Heading 3  
#### Heading 4  

**2. Text Formatting**
---
**Syntax :**  
```
**Bold** or __Bold__  
*Italic* or _Italic_  
~~Strikethrough~~       
```
**Output :**    
**Bold** or __Bold__  
*Italic* or _Italic_  
~~Strikethrough~~
