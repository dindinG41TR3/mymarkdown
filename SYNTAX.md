**1. Heading**  
---
**Syntax :**  
\# Heading 1  
\## Heading 2  
\### Heading 3  
\#### Heading 4  

**Output :**    
# Heading 1  
## Heading 2  
### Heading 3  
#### Heading 4  

**2. Text Formatting**
---
**Syntax :**  
```
**Bold**
*Italic*  
~~Strikethrough~~     
```
**Output :**    
**Bold**   
*Italic*  
~~Strikethrough~~
