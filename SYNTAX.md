# HEADING 
**Syntax :**  
```
# Heading 1  
## Heading 2  
### Heading 3  
#### Heading 4  
```
**Output :**    
# Heading 1  
## Heading 2  
### Heading 3  
#### Heading 4  

---
# TEXT FORMATTING
**Syntax :**  
```
**Bold** or __Bold__  
*Italic* or _Italic_  
~~Strikethrough~~  
__underline__  
**_Bold and Italic_**
Underline italics 	__*underline italics*__
Underline bold 	__**underline bold**__
Bold Italics 	***bold italics*** 	underline bold italics 	__***underline bold italics***__
Underline 	__underline__ 	 Strikethrough 	 ~~Strikethrough~~
```
**Output :**    
**Bold** or __Bold__  
*Italic* or _Italic_  
~~Strikethrough~~  
__underline__  
**_Bold and Italic_**
